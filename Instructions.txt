Usage
-Launch the app (double-click main.exe or run python main.py).

-Select computation

  Contrast Computation → click OK.

  SNR Computation → choose channel & ROI/Point mode, then click OK.

  Choose the folder containing your images when prompted.

  Process images

   - Contrast: click two points per measurement, press n to next image, ESC to exit.

   - SNR (ROI): click to select foreground points first, then press b to draw ROI(s), press n / ESC.

   - SNR (Points): click to select foreground points first, then press b to select background points, press n / ESC.

-Results are saved automatically in your folder:

  - Contrast

     contrast_results.xlsx

     annotated-contrast/ → {image_name}_annotated.png

  - SNR (ROI)

     snr_results_roi.xlsx

     annotated-snr-roi/

  - SNR (Points)

     snr_results_points.xlsx

     annotated-snr-points/
